## Expanding "Being Understood" through Accessibility

Speaker: [Sachi Majima](https://fukuoka.a11yconf.net/#speaker-majima)

### What is Accessibility?

- Access + ability
- Accessibility is not 0 or 100 — it is something you **improve**
- Raise it to a usable level, then broaden the range of situations in which it can be used
- [Web Accessibility Perspective Videos](https://www.w3.org/WAI/perspective-videos/)
  - Videos showcasing the benefits of a11y in various situations
- a11y is not just for people with disabilities
